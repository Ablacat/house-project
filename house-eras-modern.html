Modern Houses

1930-present

History

Modern homes started with the Bauhaus movement. This movement was based on constructing buildings with simple structures and no ornamantation. Then it changed into buildings with sleek edges and big windows. Even though these homes are 'simple' they are expensive and not everyone can afford them.

Characteristics

  • Asymmetrical.
  • Smooth walls.
  • Streamlined appearance.
  • Wraparound windows.
  • Open floor plan.
