Neo-eclectic Houses

1965-present (some were built before)

History

In the 1960s people were rebelling against modern houses and wanted houses that resembled traditional houses. So builders borrowed aspects from many styles and put on a fresh look and made what we now know as Neoeclectic houses. There is also a house type called a McMansion, these houses are oversized Neoeclectic houses that were put together hastily.

Characteristics

  • Used materials like vinyl or imitation stone.
  • Combination of historical styles.
  • Brick, stone, vinyl, and composite materials.
  • Has Neo-traditional architecture.
